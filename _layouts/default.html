Sudarshan Rajagopalan

I am a second-year PhD student in the Vision and Image Understanding (VIU) Lab at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Vishal Patel. My research focuses on computer vision, specifically algorithms for image restoration.

I completed my undergraduate studies at the Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University. I received the DAAD WISE scholarship for an internship with Prof. Eckehard Steinbach at TUM, Germany. I also worked with Prof. Kaushik Mitra at the Computational Imaging Lab, IIT Madras, on deep learning-based image restoration problems.
